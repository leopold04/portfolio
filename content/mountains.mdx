For as long as I've been alive, I've always enjoyed the challenge that comes with scaling a mountain. There's something about the crisp air and beautiful view that has you coming back time and time again.

## Mount Major, New Hampshire

**Location:** Alton, New Hampshire  
**Date:** August 2024

![Mount Major Summit](/mountain.JPG)

![Trail](/trees.JPG)
Mount Major is a mountain located in Alton, New Hampshire, south of Lake Winnipesaukee and northeast of Straightback Mountain in the Belknap Range. The scenic, rocky summit is a popular hiking destination, accessible by multiple trails including the Mount Major Trail, the Brook Trail, and the Boulder Loop.

## Mount Cardigan

**Location:** Alexandria, New Hampshire
**Date:** August 2017

![Cardigan](/P1070671.JPG)

Mount Cardigan is a prominent bare-rock summit in the towns of Orange and Alexandria in western New Hampshire, USA. While its peak is only 3,155 feet above sea level, it has extensive areas of bare granite ledges and alpine scrub, giving it the feel to hikers of a much higher mountain

## Slide Mountain, New York

**Location:** Catskill Mountain Range
**Date:** July 2021

![Slide](/IMG_5623.JPG)
Slide Mountain is the highest peak in the Catskill Mountains of the U.S. state of New York. It is located in the town of Shandaken in Ulster County
